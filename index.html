How to play

About

This is a simple game. Basically you need to get the player to the water, once there, you will get to the next round, which will add another enemy to the board. The enemies will only move left to right, on the road portion. Getting hit by an enemy will reset you to the lower left position on the board.

You get 3 lives to start, once you run out of lives, the game will reset (back to round 1, with 3 lives).

To start, be sure to click your mouse within the game board, to get the focus on the canvas.

Moving

Use your arrow keys to move:
  • right arrow to go right
  • left arrow to go left
  • up arrow to go up
  • down arrow to go down
  • esc key to reset game

You cannot move off of the board
